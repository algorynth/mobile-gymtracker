Privacy Policy for Gym Tracker

Last updated: December 20, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Gym Tracker ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application Gym Tracker (the "App").

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

The App allows you to track the following personal fitness information:

  • Body Measurements: Weight, body fat percentage, muscle mass
  • Workout Data: Exercises, sets, reps, weights lifted
  • Calorie Tracking: Daily calorie intake and goals
  • Progress Photos: Photos you take to track your fitness progress
  • User Profile: Name, age, height, fitness goals
Important: Local Storage Only
All data you enter into Gym Tracker is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers. Your information never leaves your device.

How We Use Your Information

Since all data is stored locally on your device, we do not use your personal information for any purpose other than providing the App's functionality directly to you on your device.

The App uses your data solely to:

  • Display your fitness progress and statistics
  • Calculate and show charts and trends
  • Store your workout history for your personal reference
  • Show your progress photos within the App

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third parties. Since all data is stored locally on your device, we have no access to your personal information.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics
  • Facebook SDK
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud storage services

Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device using industry-standard local database encryption. The security of your data depends on your device's security settings (screen lock, encryption, etc.).

We recommend:

  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Using a strong screen lock
  • Not sharing your device with untrusted individuals

Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data remains on your device until you choose to delete it
  • Uninstalling the App will delete all stored data
  • You can delete individual records within the App at any time

Children's Privacy

Gym Tracker is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the App
  • Modify: Edit any of your records at any time
  • Delete: Remove any or all data from the App
  • Portability: Your data is stored on your device

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
